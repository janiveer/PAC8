Getting Started

To log in to the PAC8 app:

  1. Tap the PAC8 icon on your mobile device’s home screen or applications menu.

  2. Enter your login details:

    Username

    Enter your username.

    Password

    Enter your password.

  3. Tap Login.

    The Main menu is displayed.

Note

If you have forgotten your password, tap Forgotten Password.
