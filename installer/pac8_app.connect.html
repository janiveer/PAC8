Connect to Controller

This menu enables you to connect your mobile device to a PAC8 controller via Bluetooth.

Notes

  • Bluetooth must be enabled on your mobile device to connect to a controller.

  • The controller must be within Bluetooth range of the mobile device.

  • The customer must have granted you to access the controller.
To connect to a controller:

  1. If necessary, tap Scan for Controllers to scan for controllers. (You don’t usually need to do this — the PAC8 app starts scanning for controllers as soon as this menu is displayed.)

    If any controllers are discovered, they are displayed as menu options.

  2. Tap the controller that you want to connect to.

    The Synchronisation menu is displayed.

  3. When you’re ready, tap ← to return to the Main menu.
